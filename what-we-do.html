What we do

Red Mechanic offers quality consulting expertise in inventing, creating and managing simple systems.

TLDR; We develop awesome mobile apps & web backends + do expertise research, consulting and prototyping. Quality before price. Always.

OUR MISSION

WHAT WE'VE ACHIEVED

We have developed high-end software solutions on iOS, Android, OSX and Linux. We have made electronics come to life in IoT (Internet Of Things) using Bluetooth LE/Smart. We have helped companies with Apple MFi implementation. We've made KaiOS apps. We have created indoor navigation systems. We've done many iOS apps. We can do many things.

MOBILE APPS:

  • We create native iOS apps using Swift/XCode or cross platform (iOS/Android) apps using Google's Flutter development platform. We recently started exploring the brilliant KaiOS system for feature phones, making Internet accessible for the emerging markets. We recently launched the only dating service available for KaiOS, named Datinga
  • We started creating iOS apps back in 2008 (as Agile Dimension). We had presence in the App store from day one and our first app was featured by Apple in one of their iPhone TV commercials "There's an app for that" (Compass Go). Our participation was approved by Steve Jobs, as all their ads were at that time. Due to the difficulty of keeping old apps profitable, we have closed down many of them and learned a lot over the years on how to create sustainable app ecosystems. Right now we only have these available in App store: Photosane, Fireplace, PowerAid, but more are coming.

WEB / BACKEND / MICROSERVICES

We do primarily build performant and fault tolerant distributed backend systems with Elixir (Erlang) using the Phoenix framework.

In 2006 we started building backends using Ruby on Rails. In 2016 we started our move to Elixir and we are still using it successfully.
